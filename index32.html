Brief introduction

  • Internship Duration 1 Month
  • Location (Remote Work)

Our Internship program is designed to provide you with hands on experience in the world of web development. Throughout the 1-month journey, you will have the oppournity to work on real projects, collaborate with experienced developers and contribute to cutting-edge web solutions. Whether you're a coding enthusiast or a design aficionado, this program will empower you to create impactful web experiences.

After completing the first month of your internship, you will be required to go through an interview process. If you successfully pass the interview, you will be eligible for a performance-based stipend ranging from 10,000 to 15,000 rupees. This stipend will be a reflection of your dedication, skills, and overall performance during your internship.

At the end of the internship, if you wish to apply for the interview process, there will be a registration cost of 249/-. This fee covers the administrative expenses associated with the interview process, ensuring a fair and structured evaluation of candidates. However, if you choose not to participate in the interview process, you have the option to pay 149/- and obtain your Internship Completion Letter and Recommendation Letter.

Highlights of the Internship

  • Real world Experience: Dive into actual projects and gain practical skills that will set you apart in the industry.
  • Mentorship Learn from seasoned web developers who are dedicated to guiding you through challenges and fostering your growth.
  • Collaborative Environment with a dynamic team that values creativity innovation and teamwork.
  • Skill Enhancement Hone your coding, design and problem-solving abilities under the guadiance of industry professionals.

Qualifications

  • Proficiency in HTML, CSS and Javascript
  • Familiarity with Front-end frameworks (e.g., React Js)
  • Passion for Web Development and a willingness to learn
